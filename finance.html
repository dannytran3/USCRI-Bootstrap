Accounts Payable Guide

Instructions on how to fill in out a variety forms that go to Accounts payable. This includes: Check Request Forms, Expense Reimbursement Request, Travel Advance, and Visa Request/Purchase order.

Check Request Form

A check request form is used for purchases of limited goods or services.

Expense Reimbursement Request Form

Payment to an individual for business expenses incurred on behalf of USCRI and paid with personal funds.

Visa Request Order Form

This form is to be completed each time an employee makes purchases with USCRI’s credit card. Please attach all supporting documents when turning in this form.

ADP Direct Deposit Enrollment

This form must be completed by an employee to initiate or change direct deposit elections

Federal W-4 Withholding Certificate

This certificate must be completed by an employee at hire and/or when an employee elects a change in allowances or filing status

Intern Check Request Instruction

A guide for interns on how to complete the check Request form

Intern Timesheet Expense Reimbursement

This form is to be completed by the intern to record their hours and daily travel expenses
